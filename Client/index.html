        About This Project

        E-Shopping is where innovation meets convenience. We're an online shop dedicated to bringing you the latest in electronics and essential gadgets. From powerful devices to the accessories that make them shine, our goal is to connect you with the tech you need in a simple, secure, and enjoyable way.

        ✨ Key Features
        • User Authentication — register, login, and manage profile.
        • Product Management — browse products; admins can add/delete.
        • Shopping Cart & Checkout — add/remove items and place orders.
        • Order Management — users and admins can view orders.
        • Admin Dashboard — manage products, users, and orders.
        Project Snapshot

        Tech: Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Bootstrap
        Purpose: Learning, demo, and quick prototyping
